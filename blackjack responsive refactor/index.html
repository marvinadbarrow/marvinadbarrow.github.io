How To Play ×

NOTE* in this game, ACES score 11 points. Get 20 wins to beat the house


1. Hit 'Dealer Drop' button above cards.

2.The dealer will draw TWO cards, so now it's your turn; hit the 'Player Drop' button.

3. Evaluate your cards to decide whether revealing another card will cause you to bust (anything over 21 and you BUST).

4. If you're feeling lucky, pick another card by clicking on the first available card to the right of the already revealed player cards. You can keep on pulling cards until you feel it's too risky to pull anymore without busting.

How To Play ×

5. Once you decide you want no more cards, hit the 'STAND' button. The dealer will then drop their last card, and the winner is whoever has the largest score not greater than 21. But there are other scenarios explained below.

SCENARIOS TO WATCH OUT FOR

a)THE DEALER HOLDS: - this happens when the dealer draws two cards whose combined value is greater than 19. Since the smallest card value is 2, if the dealer has more than 19 (i.e. 20 or 21) any card picked will cause them to bust - so, dealer won't drop more cards.

So you are forced to pick cards until you get outcome 'c' or 'd' on the following page.

How To Play ×

b) If you decide to 'stand', and the dealer's last card matches their score with yours, you've tied and a 'you've DRAWN' message will appear.

c) If the dealer holds on 21 points, and you then score 21, you've got a SUPER draw. Same thing happens if you score 21, hit 'STAND' and the dealer drops a card and scores 21

d) If the dealer holds on 20, and you get 20, it's a draw. If the dealer holds on 20 and you pull a card and score 21, YOU WIN. But if the dealer holds on 21 and you score 20, YOU LOSE.
