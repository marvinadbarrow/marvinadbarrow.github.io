How To Play ×

1. Hit 'Begin Game' button at upper left just above the higher set of cards.

2.The dealer has dealt, so now it's your turn; hit the 'Your Deal' button to the right of the 'begin game' button.

3. Evaluate your cards to decide whether revealing another card will cause you to bust (getting a card value of more than 21).

4. if you're feeling lucky, pick another card (or more than one) by clicking on the first available card to the right of the cards you already pulled. You can keep on pulling cards until you feel it's too risky to pull anymore without busting.

How To Play ×

5. Once you decide you want no more cards, hit the 'STAND' button. The dealer will then drop their last card the winner is whoever has the largest card value (as long as they haven't bust).

SCENARIOS TO WATCH OUT FOR

a)THE DEALER HOLDS: - this happens when the dealer draws two cards whose combined value is greater than 19. Since the smallest card is 2, if the dealer has more than 19 (i.e. 20 or 21) any card picked will cause them to bust - so, dealer won't drop more cards.

So you are forced to pick cards until you either bust, score 21, or run out of cards; if you're still in the game after picking 5 cards, you win automatically with a 'five card trick'.

How To Play ×

b) If you decide to 'stand', and the dealer's last card gets them the same score as you, then you have tied with the dealer and a 'you've DRAWN' message will appear.

c) If the dealer holds on 21 points, and you then score 21, the game stops, and you get a SUPER draw. Same thing happens if you score 21, hit 'STAND' and the dealer drops a card and scores 21

d) If the dealer holds on 20, and you pull a card and score 21, YOU WIN. But if the dealer scores 21 and you score 20, you lose, because, cruelly, I've not allowed you to stand once the dealer holds: SORRY! :)
